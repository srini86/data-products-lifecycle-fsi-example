Data Product Description:
Retail Customer Churn Risk - Unified view of churn risk for retail banking customers
Demand LOB Alignment:
Retail Banking Division
Request Date, Stakeholder:
2024-01-15, Sarah Mitchell (Head of Retail Customer Analytics)

1. Business Problem Statement

What is the business problem we are addressing?
Retail Banking lacks a unified, trusted view of customer churn risk:
  • Multiple data silos across teams
  • High-risk customers identified too late
  • No explainability for regulators
  • Manual, spreadsheet-based risk scoring

2. Business Value

What is the business value we derive by solving?
  • Reduce churn from 8.5% → 6.0%
  • Save NZD $12M annual revenue at risk
  • Protect 2,400 high-risk customers
  • 20% improvement in campaign response
  • Reduce time to intervention: 45 → 7 days

3. Risk / Impediments

What happens if we don't solve it?
  • Digital channel data quality varies
  • Compliance may require model changes
  • Branch managers may not trust scores
  • Continued customer attrition to competitors
  • Regulatory scrutiny on unexplainable decisions

4. KPI's

How will we measure the product ROI/effectiveness?
Primary:
  • Churn Rate: 8.5% → 6.0%
  • Early Warning Accuracy: 75% precision
Guardrails:
  • False Positive Rate < 30%
  • 100% predictions explainable

5. Key Stakeholders

Business Impact & Target Audience
Owner: Sarah Mitchell (Analytics)
Consumers:
  • Retention Analytics Team (Daily)
  • Branch Relationship Managers (Weekly)
  • Marketing Campaign Team (Monthly)
  • Executive Leadership (Board reports)

6. Key Entities

Data Domains / Systems in Play
Primary Entity: Customer (1 row per retail customer)

Key Attributes:
  • churn_risk_score (0-100)
  • risk_tier (High/Medium/Low)
  • primary_risk_drivers
  • recommended_intervention

7. Upstream Sources

What are the external/internal data sources?
  • Core Banking (T24) - Customer, accounts, transactions
  • CRM (Salesforce) - Interactions, complaints
  • Digital (Amplitude) - App usage, engagement
  • Complaints (ServiceNow) - Issues, sentiment

8. Downstream Dependencies

What is impacted & how will we address change?
  • Retention campaign targeting systems
  • Branch dashboards & CRM integration
  • Executive reporting & BI tools
  • ML model feature store

Change: Versioned contracts, backward compatibility

9. Impact on Org Goals

How will this affect the Organization?
  • Supports customer retention strategy
  • Enables data-driven decision making
  • Reduces regulatory risk (explainability)
  • Demonstrates value of data platform
  • Cross-team collaboration model

10. Solution Approach, Stakeholder Experience & Considerations

How are we thinking about solving the problem? Alternatives, Considerations, Solution Bias
Approach: Build a governed data product on Snowflake combining behavioral signals, transactional patterns, and engagement data to produce explainable risk scores.

Key Considerations: Daily refresh by 6 AM UTC • 24-month rolling history • Excludes business customers, new customers (<30 days), deceased/fraud accounts

Stakeholder Experience: Full PII for Retention Team • Masked PII for Branch Managers • Aggregated segments for Marketing • Metrics only for Executives
